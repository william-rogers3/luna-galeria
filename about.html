Andrés Luna

Galería de Luna is an online portfolio of all the Visual Art that Cleveland Filmmaker/Photographer Andrés Luna has created, and is a way to display the many mediums that he has to offer when it comes to storytelling through imagery.

Andrés Luna is currently a student of the School of Film and Media Arts in Cleveland State University's College of Liberal Arts and Social Sciences. Entering his Junior year, he has decided to showcase his portfolio and offer his services in a way that is accessible to people who are interested in collaborating with him, and thus, Galería de Luna was born.
